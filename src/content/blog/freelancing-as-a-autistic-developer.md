## Why Productized Services are Great for Autistic Entrepreneurs

Productized services are really easy to sell, and you can systematize everything.

When I was billing hourly I was always second guessing what I can bill or and not bill for because I don't want to seem like I was just running up a fee

Productized services are very narrow in scope, so you know what you can charge for.

A fixed price for a fixed scope then allows you to templatize, systematize and optimize your work, this means that your effective hourly rate is massive

These are some of the things you can templatize, they include but are not limited to:

- Websites

- Contracts

- Workflows

- Onboarding flows

- Email Outreach

If you can templatize it, you can automate it. I am now creating Fully automated, productized services for my clients

I am using OpenClaw for this end. For example I am creating a productized analytics insights service that helps Optimize your marketing and your sales
