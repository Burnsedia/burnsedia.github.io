# Weekly Conversion Review

## Cadence
- Weekly, 30 minutes.
- Owner: Bailey Burnsed.

## Scorecard

Track week-over-week:

- `newsletter_submit` total
- Newsletter conversion rate by page (`newsletter_submit / sessions`)
- `github_sponsor_click` total and CTR
- `patreon_click` total and CTR
- `social_follow_click` total and top platform
- `work_with_me_click` total and top source
- Returning visitor rate

## Segment Views

- By `source`
- By `placement`
- By `page_type`

## Decision Rules

- If newsletter conversion rate drops more than 15% for 2 weeks, test CTA placement before copy changes.
- If sponsor/patreon clicks drop more than 20% for 2 weeks, move support block higher on home and blog pages.
- If work-with-me clicks drop more than 20% for 2 weeks, review CTA hierarchy outside `/service`.
- If one placement contributes less than 2% of conversions for 3 weeks, replace or remove it.

## Weekly Output

- Top 3 winning placements
- Top 3 underperforming placements
- One change to ship next week
- One thing to stop doing
