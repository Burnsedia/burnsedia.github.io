# Growth Dashboard Specification

## Objective
Track what matters weekly without overcomplicating analytics.

## North Star
Qualified Visits from organic search and social to priority pages.

## KPI Groups

### Awareness
- Search impressions (GSC)
- Social impressions
- Net new followers (X and GitHub)

### Interest
- Search clicks and CTR
- Social engagement rate
- Profile visits

### Consideration
- Engaged sessions (GA4)
- Average engagement time
- Returning visitor ratio

### Conversion
- Newsletter signups
- Waitlist or lead submissions
- Conversion rate from qualified visits

## Weekly Dashboard Fields
- Week start and end
- Blog posts published
- Social posts published
- GSC impressions/clicks/CTR/position
- Social impressions/engagement/link clicks/follower net
- GA4 sessions and engaged sessions
- Conversions and conversion rate
- Notes and confidence level

## Alert Rules
- CTR down >20% for 2 weeks on high-impression pages -> rewrite title/meta/intro.
- Qualified visits down >25% WoW -> inspect top losing pages and channels.
- Net followers <= 0 for 2 weeks -> run collaboration and proof-content sprint.
- Conversion rate down >30% for 2 weeks -> simplify CTA path and landing copy.

## Attribution Approach
- Last non-direct touch as default attribution.
- Add basic UTM tags to all social links.
- Track assist notes manually for significant touchpoints.

## Reporting Cadence
- Weekly: KPI update and tactical decisions.
- Monthly: retrospective with scale/kill/iterate decisions.

## Monthly Retro Template
- What improved and why.
- What declined and why.
- Top 3 winning topics/formats.
- Top 3 weak pages/channels.
- Decisions for next month.
