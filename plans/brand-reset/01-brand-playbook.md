# Brand Playbook

## Mission
Build useful apps, weird games, and open systems in public while staying radically authentic.

## Brand Positioning
I am an autistic hacker-builder shipping products in public.

- Primary identity: product builder and creator.
- Secondary identity: educator through build logs and open-source receipts.
- Revenue support: limited productized services to fund app and game development.

## Core Audience

### Primary
- Indie hackers and technical solo founders with operations chaos.

### Secondary
- Neurodivergent developers and creators who want sustainable workflows.

### Tertiary
- Nerdy developers interested in Godot, Neovim, and open-source systems.

## Signature Pillars
- Godot game and tool development.
- Neovim-first engineering workflows.
- Build in public with real metrics, tradeoffs, and postmortems.

## Brand Values
- Ownership over dependence.
- Clarity over hype.
- Systems over heroics.
- Consistency over bursts.
- Honesty over polished pretending.

## Voice Rules

### Do
- Use first-person language.
- Show concrete details: commands, metrics, code decisions.
- Include mistakes and reversals.
- Keep tone direct, practical, and slightly rebellious.

### Do Not
- Use generic startup cliches.
- Publish advice disconnected from real weekly work.
- Hide service-vs-product tension.

## Messaging Hierarchy

### Home
Autistic hacker-builder shipping apps, games, and tools in public.

### About
Personal operating philosophy, constraints, and why this work matters.

### Projects
What is live now, current status, and what is next.

### Blog
Build logs, systems, technical notes, and postmortems.

### Service
Limited productized sprint offer with hard scope boundaries.

## CTA Hierarchy
- Primary CTA: Read the build log.
- Secondary CTA: Join the newsletter.
- Tertiary CTA: Follow on GitHub and X.

## Fit and Anti-Fit

### Good Fit
- Builders who value practical systems and open documentation.
- Teams/founders who want specific automation outcomes.

### Not a Fit
- Clients seeking open-ended custom agency work.
- Buyers expecting unlimited revisions and vague deliverables.

## One-Liner Variants
- Autistic hacker-builder shipping Godot games, Neovim workflows, and open-source products in public.
- I build apps, games, and automation systems in public, then share the receipts.
